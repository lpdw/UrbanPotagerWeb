Urban Potager is an open-source automated indoor garden developed by UrbanaPlant, in partnership with the FacLab and LetMeKnow. The goal of this project is to design and create a compact garden which can be installed in any household that doesn't have an outdoor space for growing plants (garden, terrace, balcony) with little sunlight exposure.
